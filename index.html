Point of Sales

🎯 Welcome to Sales

You're all set to process customer transactions! Here's how to make a sale:

  • Select Services: Click on services your customer wants (left side)
  • Adjust Quantities: Change quantity if customer wants multiple services
  • Apply Discount (Optional): Enter discount amount if available
  • Choose Payment: Select payment method (Cash, M-Pesa, Card, Cheque)
  • Complete Sale: Click "Complete Sale" to finalize and record transaction
  • Print Receipt: Click "Print" to generate and print professional receipt

✨ Tip: After printing, the system automatically prepares for your next customer!

Price Management

💰 Welcome to Price Management

Control your pricing strategy with built-in approval system to prevent unfair pricing. Here's how it works:

  • Add Services: Create new services with names and prices in KES
  • Set Categories: Organize services (Haircut, Shave, Treatment, etc.)
  • Propose Price Changes: Submit new prices for staff approval
  • Require 2 Approvals: At least 2 staff members must approve price changes
  • Prevent Bias: No single person can unfairly change prices
  • Track History: See all price changes and who approved them

⚠️ Important: Price changes help your business grow, but must be fair to all. The approval system ensures transparency!

Note: Price changes require approval from at least 2 registered staff members to prevent pricing bias.

Price Approvals

✅ Your Approval Power

As a registered staff member, you have the power to approve or reject price changes. This helps keep pricing fair!

  • Review Requests: Manager proposes new prices, you review them
  • Approve Fairly: Click "Approve" if the new price seems reasonable
  • Reject if Unfair: Click "Reject" if the price increase is too high
  • Your Vote Matters: Need 2 approvals total to change prices
  • No Self-Approval: Can't approve your own proposed prices (prevents fraud)
  • See Changes: Once approved, new prices appear in Sales tab

💡 Remember: Fair pricing keeps customers happy and business growing!

Sales Reports

📊 Welcome to Sales Analytics

Track your business performance with real-time reports and insights. Here's what you can see:
